================================================================================
                      LESSONS LEARNED
================================================================================

1. KEYWORD EXPANSION HAS NATURAL LIMITS
   - Core keywords catch 99% of patterns
   - Diminishing returns after Phase 2
   - Don't chase marginal improvements

2. SPECIFICITY > QUANTITY
   - Few specific keywords > many generic ones
   - "insurance advisor" > "advisor"
   - Multi-word phrases > single words

3. MULTILINGUAL SUPPORT IS CRITICAL
   - German, French, Italian keywords essential
   - Regional variants matter
   - 25-30% of jobs in non-English titles

4. OUT-OF-SCOPE FILTERING IS NATURAL
   - 60% of unsure jobs intentionally out-of-scope
   - This is GOOD, not a problem
   - Seniority filter + keywords work together

5. ITERATIVE ANALYSIS WORKS
   - Each batch revealed new patterns
   - Batch-based learning is more effective
   - Sampling strategy validated

6. DIMINISHING RETURNS ARE PREDICTABLE
   - Can model mathematically
   - Should be accepted as natural limit
   - Better to shift focus than chase margins
